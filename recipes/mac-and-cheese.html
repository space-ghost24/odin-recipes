Mac and Cheese

Description

Ditch the boxed stuff with this easy to make mac and cheese. With just a handful of ingredients it can be ready faster than boxed macaroni.

Ingredients

  • 6oz macaroni
  • water to cover
  • 5oz can evaporated milk
  • 1oz heavy cream
  • 6oz grated colby cheese

Instructions

  1. Medium skillit in high heat, add noodles and water, bring to boil
  2. Stir until water mostly evaorates, add cream anc cheese and mix
