Baked Chicken Thighs

Description

These easy baked chicken thighs require minimum preparation; I love this quick recipe because I don't like complicated recipes! Feel free to season the chicken with your favorite spices. I like to use cayenne powder and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 4 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 tsp onion powder
  • Other spices optional

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375f
  2. Place thighs in baking dish, season both sides
  3. Bake for 30 mins or until internal temp 165f
